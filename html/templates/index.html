For my project I made a Wake-Up clock, I have called this Easy Wake-Up. On the image you see a photo of my project. The lamp can be switched on / off by hand gesture holding 3 s long before the Easy Wake-Up. You can also turn the wake-up clock off when it's playing with the same gesture.
